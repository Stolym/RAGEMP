Usage: clientdbinfo cldbid={clientDBID}
       clientdbinfo cldbid={clientDBID}|cldbid={clientDBID}

Permissions:
  b_virtualserver_client_dbinfo

Description:
  Displays detailed database information about a client including unique ID,
  creation date, etc.

Example:
  clientdbinfo cldbid=4
  client_unique_identifier=FPMPSC6MXqXq751dX7BKV0JniSo= client_nickname=ScP client_created=1265411019 ...
  error id=0 msg=ok
